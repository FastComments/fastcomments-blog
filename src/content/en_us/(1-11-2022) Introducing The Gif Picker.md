### How to Use It

<p>If image uploads are enabled, a GIF icon will show up in the comment toolbar.</p>
<p>Clicking this will display a list of trending gifs. This list preloads a certain number of images and allows for infinite scrolling.</p>

### Gif Providers

Currently, the only integration that FastComments supports is with Giphy.

### Gif Anonymization

We understand that a significant portion of our user base chooses FastComments due to privacy reasons.

All assets and API calls from Giphy are devoid of information such as cookies and your users' IP address.

### Gif Content Rating

The content rating of gifs can be adjusted to range from PG to R-rated based on your
audience. [Documentation for customizing the content rating is here.](https://docs.fastcomments.com/guide-customizations-and-configuration.html#gif-rating)

### Documentation

There is specific documentation for the gif picker. See here: https://docs.fastcomments.com/guide-gifs.html

### In Conclusion

We've made a significant investment to ensure that the Gif picker and its related functionality are fast and do not slow down the
rest of the product.

Like the rest of FastComments, we hope that you find this feature fast, fun, and user-friendly.
